Absolute Reality

Absolute Reality has three aspects:

  1. Absolute Abstract Space-Root of the quality which makes all forms possible-Sub-stratum of matter. Stepped down to physical matter-our bodies.
  2. Duration-Root of Time. From the principle of order emerges into manifestation.
  3. Absolute Abstract Motion-Pre-cosmic ideation. Root of that quality which makes creativity possible. Root of individual consciousness. Stepped down manifests our mind-our thought.
