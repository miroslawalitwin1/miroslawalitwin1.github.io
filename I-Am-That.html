I Am That

The diagram bellow is a simplified picture of esoteric and exoteric constitution of Reality on all planes.

1.The dark brown, small and rotating cube in the centre represents you as a being written into the square of space time. [ It can also represent universe].

2.The two pyramids piercing the small cube are two major living Forces: a. the light brown pyramid-force pushing from bottom upwards; b. the light violet pyramid-divine force descending and pushing from above downwards. This is exactly what you experience in the life of Nature and yourself on the path of Sadhana.

3.The dark orange circle represents your Personal God – with attributes like Omnipresent, Omniscient, Almighty – Saguna Brahman.

4.The dark grey, large and rotating cube-frame behind the orange circle represents Dark and hostile Forces and Powers adverse to Nature and human beings. They are Evil Spirits and Brothers of Left Path.

5.The very large dotted circle represents Impersonal God – without name, without attributes, Ineffable, Unknowable – Nirguna Brahman. As you can see its space-dots pervade and permeate all previous entities. It is indeed that worlds are in Him, move in Him, and have in Him their being.

Please notice a little light dot at the centre of the little dark brown cube – it is your Eternal Self. It is God in you. It is your true Life and true heritage. YOU ARE THAT – if you recoil yourself from identifying with your body, mind, personality, ego. If you will try to identify yourself with your Eternal Self – which is Brahman – you will reach not only Self-realisation, but also God-realisation. You will touch Immortality which is beyond inner self and you will enter Eternity which is beyond this space-time. If you persevere in purification of body, mind and ego [cause of dualities], and make them faithful instruments of your Spirit, instead of masters as they are now; you will reclaim your birthright and you will be THAT.
