Chakras

Chakras are vital energy wheels connecting physical body with subtle bodies. The most known are 7 chakras as follows:

1.Root Chakra-Muladhara-Survival

Located at the base of spine. Color:red. Element:Earth. Four petals. Bija mantra:Lam. Crystal:red jasper. Musical note:C. Metal:Lead. Planet:Earth.

2.Sacral Chakra-Svadisthana-Emotions

Located below the navel. Color:orange. Element:Water. Six petals. Bija mantra:Vam. Crystal:orange carnelian. Musical note:D. Metal:Tin. Planets:Mercury, Moon.

3.Solar Chakra-Manipura-Vitality

Located just below the sternum. Color:yellow. Element:Fire. Ten petals. Bija mantra:Ram. Crystal:yellow jasper. Musical note:E. Metal:Iron. Planets:Sun, Mars.

4.Heart Chakra-Anahata-Love

Located at thymus gland/heart area. Color:green. Element:Air. Twelve petals. Bija mantra:Yam. Crystal:green aventure. Musical note:F. Metal:Copper. Planet:Venus. Its symbol is comprised of two triangles-superimposed on each other-one facing upwards, the other facing downwards. It is the middle point of the path-the gate to the true knowledge.

5.Throat Chakra-Vishuddha-Creativity

Located at the thyroid gland. Color:blue. Element:Space. Sixteen petals. Bija mantra:Ham. Crystal:blue lace agate. Musical note:G. Metal:Mercury. Planet:Mercury.

6.Brow Chakra-Ajna/Third Eye/-Intuition

Located between the eyebrows. Color:indigo. Element:Light. Ninety six petals. Bija mantra:Aum/Om. Crystal:sodalite. Musical note:A. Metal:Silver. Planets:Moon, Saturn. The place of meeting of Ida and Pingala nadis-represents duality in all things.

7.Crown Chakra-Sahasrara-Cosmic Insight

Located at the top of the head. Color:violet/white. Element:Emptiness. Thousand petals. Bija mantra:Silence or Om. Crystal:ametyst. Musical note:B. Metal:Gold. Planets:Universe.

New Age Chakras

There are 5 more important chakras. Four located one above the other, above the head-and one below the root chakra. These chakras are 4-th and 5-th dimension chakras and are used in spiritual healing.

  • Transcendence of Time Chakra
  • Soul Connection Chakra
  • Earth Connection Chakra
  • Mind over Matter Chakra
  • Universal Unity Chakra

The chakras are very often unbalanced or blocked, which is the cause of various illnesses. Basically-to unlock chakra-it has to be done by professional person, but you can give it a go by meditation,crystals nad bijas. Bija is a specific sound/vibration/ which aligns the chakras. It should be performed from mentally repeating the sound in mind to uttering with a very loud voice. You can also balance the chakras by asanas/postures/ of Hatha Yoga, and by pranayamas/breathing/ into that particular chakra.
